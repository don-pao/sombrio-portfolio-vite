// CONTEXT: PROBLEM & OBJECTIVE

MM Boxing Academy needed a professional, aggressive online presence that matched the intensity of the gym. The goal was to consolidate class scheduling, membership sign-ups, and merchandise sales into one easy-to-manage platform.

// SOLUTION EXECUTION

This project was fully developed using the **Wix** platform, leveraging its e-commerce and booking capabilities. The design focused on large, high-impact visuals and the use of the academy's signature red and black palette to convey power and discipline. The Wix editor was chosen for rapid development and ease of client handoff.

KEY FEATURES

  • Integrated class scheduling and booking system.
  • E-commerce store for branded apparel.
  • Mobile-first design for gym-goers on the move.
